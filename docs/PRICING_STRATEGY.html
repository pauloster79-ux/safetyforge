Horizon 2 Feature Strategy — Include, Don’t Upsell
Recommendation: Include daily logs, time tracking, and quality in existing tiers at no extra cost. The product vision says “one tool, not six.” Charging per-module contradicts this. Including H2 features justifies current prices, drives the app consolidation metric (target: 2+ tools replaced), and reduces churn because multi-feature users never leave.

Current Pricing Assessment

Strengths

  • Per-project + unlimited users. The moat that widens with every horizon. Carlos the laborer clocking in should never be a line-item debate.
  • $299 replaces $1,275+ in tools. As H2 features ship, the value gap becomes absurd.
  • AI bundled, not add-on. Voice, video, photo hazard, document generation — all included.
  • Enormous pricing headroom. Even a H3 price increase to $349 is 1/10th of Procore.

Weaknesses

  1. $299→$599 gap (2x) — reduces to 1.67x at proposed $499.
  2. Doc limits feel tight — Free (3) and Starter (10) below competitor free tiers.
  3. No H2 pricing plan. Need to decide before features ship.
  4. Annual discount (20%) below SaaS average (28%).
Risk Analysis
Risk 1: Giving Away H2 Features Leaves Money on the Table Mitigation: Multi-feature users churn 3-5x less. The real play is retention + justified price increase at H3. “We added $500/mo of value and raised the price $50.”
Risk 2: Per-Project Limits Revenue Per Customer Mitigation: Platform consolidation means ARPU ceiling is much higher than safety-only. Pricing headroom exists — $349-549 at H3 is still 1/10th of Procore.
Risk 3: Competitors Bundle Faster Mitigation: SafetyCulture is horizontal (losing $36M/yr, CEO turnover). Safesite is insurance-focused. Neither has voice-first field experience. Data moat compounds daily.
Risk 4: Procore Moves Downmarket Mitigation: Procore is office-first, not field-first. Their minimum viable product requires PM overhead a 25-person crew doesn’t need. Different user, different product.
Risk 5: AI Commoditization Mitigation: AI generation is the wedge, not the moat. Five compounding layers: knowledge graph, bilingual voice model, data network, workflow embedding, ecosystem lock-in.

Go-to-Market Pricing by Horizon

H1 Launch Phase (Months 1-6)

  • Lead with safety: “AI Safety Director for $99/month.”
  • 14-day Professional trial, no credit card.
  • 30% annual discount for first 500 subscribers.

H2 Expansion Phase (Months 6-12)

  • Announce ops features as “included at no extra cost.”
  • Drive consolidation: “Cancel ClockShark. Cancel Raken.”
  • Track app consolidation rate (target: 2+ tools replaced).
  • Track features per customer (target: 3.5+ modules).

H3 Platform Phase (Months 12-18)

  • Evaluate price increase once RFIs & scheduling ship.
  • Enterprise sales motion for 20+ project contractors.
  • GC-driven adoption — free GC portal, monetize through subs.
